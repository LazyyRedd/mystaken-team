About MYSTAKEN

Forged in Competition. United by Passion.

Founded in 2021, MYSTAKEN Esports has grown into a global esports organisation competing at the highest levels of competitive gaming. Our mission is to build championship-caliber teams while fostering a community of dedicated gamers and content creators.

We believe in the power of esports to bring people together, create opportunities, and push the boundaries of competitive excellence. Our teams compete across multiple titles, with a primary focus on Call of Duty.

Our Journey

Achievements & Milestones

August 2020

The Beginning

MYSTAKEN social media presence established, laying the foundation for what would become a global esports organisation.

2021

Official Launch

MYSTAKEN Esports officially established as a competitive esports organisation with our first roster.

January 2022

1 Year Anniversary

Celebrated one year of MYSTAKEN with community giveaways and expanded our team roster.

March 2025

2025 Major 2 Roster

Announced our competitive roster for the 2025 Major 2 tournament in Dallas.

2025

Content Creator Expansion

Opened applications for content creators to join the MYSTAKEN family and help grow our community.

June 23 - June 29, 2025

T8 Placement

Achieved T8 placement with roster: Beet, Wadey, TxteZy, CallumKhaos, Rico, Kiosku, Audacity, Eros.
